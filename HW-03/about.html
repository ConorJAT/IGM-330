About Audio Visualizers!

Tired of just listening to audio? Ever wish you could see audio? Now you can with audio visualization! Audio visualization allows us to capture data from various audio sources and translate that data into a visual representation of what we hear. Audio visualization can take on many forms and and cover a wide array of audio sources! For an example, click "See the App"!
